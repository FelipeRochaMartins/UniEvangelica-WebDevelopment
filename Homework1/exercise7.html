Understanding White Space

This is a paragraph. Even though this text is on a new line in the HTML, it will appear on the same line in the browser.

This is a new paragraph.
This text will appear on a new line because of the br tag.
And this will be on another new line.

Multiple spaces between words will be treated as a single space by the browser.
